Profile

Ayaka Yabuki

Singer-songwriter, Director
From Japan

シンガーソングライター、映像監督。

4歳からクラッシックピアノをはじめ、中⾼ではバンド活動を経験。その後2020年より⼊塾した⾳楽塾voiceにて作詞作曲活動を開始し、フォーク、ロック、ソウルなど幅広いジャンルの⾳楽を吸収。 現在では慶應義塾⼤学にて出会った井澤岳野⽒にも師事。
どこか映像的で、いつまでも透明さが残る、独特な楽曲世界を広げている。

また、⼤学時代携わった演劇製作の経験を⽣かしながら、⾃⾝の楽曲数本のMV監督も務めている。 今年春、新作公開予定。

Singer-songwriter and filmmaker.
She began studying classical piano at the age of four and later developed her musical voice through band activities during her teenage years. In 2020, she started writing and composing at the music school Voice Tokyo, drawing from a wide spectrum of influences including folk, alternative rock, and soul.
Her music unfolds as a cinematic landscape intimate yet expansive marked by a lingering sense of translucence and emotional clarity. She continues to shape this distinctive sonic world while studying under Takeya Izawa, whom she met at Keio University.
Alongside her music practice, she directs her own visual works, informed by her background in theatre production during her university years.
A new release is scheduled for this spring.
